QueryKey

A rationalist social network you run locally from your own desktop — and, underneath, a personal relationship manager, a lightweight CRM, and a JIRA-style task tracker for your own life. A local AI agent helps you keep up with the people and commitments in your life — while respecting your privacy and theirs.

The name comes from the Q / K / V (query, key, value) projections of a transformer's attention matrix. Built on a local-first ingest → knowledge-graph engine. The project is early; the repository has the honest, up-to-date status.
